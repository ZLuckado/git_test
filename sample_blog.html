Toads are a menace

The Problem

To understand the reasoning for toad extinction, one must truly experience the evils of their species.

Not only are they godless heathens, they are also prone to drug use.

This problem has gone unmentioned and frankly ignored by the powers that be, while the lower rungs of society suffer at the tongues of this amphibious menace.

Why exactly are toads so troublesome?

  • They are ubiquitous
  • They carry diseases (we think)
  • They contribute nothing to the good fight.
  • They eat all of the tastiest insects

So what are we to do?

We must petiton our governments to allocate funding for the eradication of these banes of human existence .

What steps must we take to inact change?

  1. First we must prepare individually for the coming invasion. This is the most important step, in case none of the others are successful.
  2. We must write an open letter to the leaders of all world governments demanding their assistance.
  3. We must then organize marches in the capitals of these countries to show our determination and our strength of numbers. Only this will instill fear and show them the seriousness of this issue.
